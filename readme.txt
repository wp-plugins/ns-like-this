This plugin allows your users to save their favorite content, and keep track of their favorites (add or remove), other user's favorites and clicks on favorites.

== Description ==

Get control over your content, check what your users most like with NS Like This, a plugin that allow your users to save their favorite posts, pages, categories or tags, and review it in their favorite private page, automatically created and managed. They can also add an emblem to make the like more eye-catching.

You can track if the users do click in their favorites using the favorites page. Also, they can check what other users most like, making the *like* more social.

The plugin also adds 3 widgets:

*	Most liked content
*	Latest liked content
*	Most visited liked content

Translation ready (i18n), now includes full translation to:

*	English (default).
*	Italian.

If you have a new translation for your language, please send us by email and we'll add it to the package.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the contents of the zip file (`ns-like-this folder`) to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Edit the settings under the section 'Settings > NS Like This' on your Wordpress admin panel.
